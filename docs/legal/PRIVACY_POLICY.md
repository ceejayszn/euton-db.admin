# Privacy Policy

## 1. Data Collection
We collect business operation data, device IDs, IP addresses, and user activity logs necessary to provide our SaaS Platform.

## 2. Data Usage
Data is used exclusively to provide analytics, ensure system health, and secure the platform against unauthorized access. We do not sell your data.

## 3. Data Security
All data is stored securely on cloud-managed PostgreSQL databases with encryption in transit.
